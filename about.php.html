About Us


HISTORY

Agbenu Bridge College is a model secondary school established in 2008 to provide qualitative secondary education. The school was birthed to be part of the correction to the growing social vices associated with falling standard of education in Nigeria; the non performing public schools resulting in establishment of high priced standard secondary school/lowly priced ones which are merely external examination malpractices centers cumulating in schools increasingly becoming character destroyers rather builders. The schools was thus conceptualized to be a first bridge for the child form the home to the school, to the society, to the tertiary institutions and return, a school which is a childâ€™s tripod of being an effective and efficient member of the home, the society and tertiary institutions as clearly articulated in her vision and mission statements. The school uses modern teaching methods with an individual gadget for teaching.

Her primary objective is to provide standard education at affordable cost that would breed disciplined and functional members of the society as secondary school graduates who can competitively stand with their contemporaries anywhere in the world. Persons with understanding and courage to act on their beliefs with the fear of the Almighty God. A Bridge indeed

She started with twenty-three students, six teachers, Mr. Stephen Ebute as the principal and Mr. Emmanuel Aba, Johnson as the coordinator. Today the school is headed by a college administrator, Mr. Oche, Michael Ogaba assisted by an assistant college administrator Mr. Audu Anthony Owoicho.
