Agbanu Bridge College is a model primary and secondary school established in 2008 to provide qualitative secondary education. The school was birthed to be part of the corrections to the alarming geometrically growing social vices associated with the falling standard of education in Otukpo town and the country as a whole and also insufficient parenting; breakdown in community and extended family
