School Sporting Activities

The School Games currently encompasses various sport activities. Sport and physical exercise are an integral part of the educational system. Compulsory teaching of physical education (PE) and extracurricular sports activities imply concepts of physical exercise and sport. Teaching PE is regular and not optional. Every pupil/student is encouraged to participate in at least one sport, and trainers/coaches are always on ground to guide and to train them.
